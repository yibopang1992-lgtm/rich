# Yangjia Emotion Framework

Use this reference when analyzing A-share short-term rotation, leader stocks,
catch-up candidates, position sizing, emotional-cycle risk, or next-session
plans. It is a research framework, not investment advice.

## Core Principle

Analyze opportunity from赚钱效应 and risk from亏钱效应. Prediction is secondary;
adaptation is primary. Prefer actions that keep the trader主动 and comfortable
under changing market conditions.

Key operating rules:

- 买入机会，卖出风险。
- 买入分歧，卖出一致。
- 人气所向，牛股所在。
- 热点来自题材，持续性来自赚钱效应。
- 判断为末，应变为本。
- 心中无顶底、无成本、无持股，只问是否继续看好。
- 不赚看不懂的钱；空仓 is a valid high-skill decision.

## Emotion Cycle

Classify the market into one of six phases before discussing sectors or
catch-up trades:

| Phase | Market Signs | Position Bias | Strategy |
|---|---|---:|---|
| 冰点 | Few limit-ups, many limit-downs, height collapse, no mainline | 0-10% | Observe or tiny test only; do not bottom-fish blindly |
| 启动 | Panic stops, first boards increase, 2-3 board space leader appears | 20-30% | Confirm new leader; first divergence entry only |
| 发酵 | Mainline confirmed, 3-5 board leaders, money-making effect spreads | 50-80% | Focus on leader/core; divergence low-buy or strong relay |
| 高潮 | Leader accelerates, huge consensus, rear stocks surge together | Reduce | Sell consistency; do not chase rear acceleration |
| 分歧 | Leader opens/炸板, high-level losses, rear stocks weaken | <=30% | Keep only true leader/core, remove weak followers |
| 退潮 | Leader breaks and A-kills, limit-downs spread, height collapses | 0% | Empty or nearly empty; no rebound gambling |

Default cycle heuristics:

- 冰点买 is not blind bottom-fishing; only test when panic shows a reversal signal.
- 高潮卖 means reduce into一致, especially after broad rear-stock catch-up.
- 分歧买 only applies inside a still-valid mainline with leader premium.
- 退潮期 has veto power over all catch-up candidates.

## Daily Five Checks

Every daily review should inspect:

1. 连板高度: space leader board count and whether height is expanding or collapsing.
2. 涨停/炸板/封板率: limit-up count, board-open rate, seal quality.
3. 大面/跌停家数: -8% big-loss count and limit-down count.
4. 主线持续性: whether sector money-making effect lasts beyond one day.
5. 龙头状态: leader turnover, acceleration, divergence, break, VWAP/premium.

If exact data is unavailable, state the data gap and use available proxies such
as amount heat, sector return, leader premium, and breadth.

## Mainline And Sector Rules

Classify a theme as one of:

- 主流: sustained money-making effect, leader premium, breadth, turnover, and
  repeated intraday repair after divergence.
- 次主流: explosive but weaker persistence; may trade only with very strong
  risk/reward.
- 支流/非主流: treat as short-lived. Prefer light observation or no trade.

Do not analyze a stock in isolation. Always compare:

- Its sector stage.
- Same-sector leaders, trend anchors, 20cm cores, and rear candidates.
- Whether another new hotspot is absorbing marginal funds.
- Whether the candidate is pure to the reviewed sector or only adjacent.

## Catch-up Candidate Rules

A catch-up candidate is valid only when:

- The mainline or heat sector is still alive.
- Leaders/core names keep premium and do not break together.
- The candidate has sufficient liquidity/amount.
- Recent gains are not already accelerated.
- Sector purity is acceptable.
- It shows relative strength versus other rear names.

Reject or downgrade when:

- The sector is in climax-next-day profit-taking mode.
- Leaders fail VWAP or lose prior-session premium.
- Candidate lagged badly during broad sector strength.
- New hotspot is stronger and old rear stocks weaken.
- Money-flow data is missing and only amount heat supports the idea.

When only free成交金额 data exists, label results as “amount-heat catch-up”,
not fund-flow-confirmed catch-up.

## Position Sizing

Use confidence and market phase to cap position suggestions in research plans:

| Win Probability | Research Position Bias |
|---:|---|
| <60% | Observe / no trade |
| 60-70% | Small test only |
| 70-80% | Medium |
| 80-90% | Heavy only in 发酵 with strong leader premium |
| >90% | Rare exceptional case; must have high upside and tight invalidation |

Hard caps:

- 冰点: 0-10%.
- 启动: 20-30%.
- 发酵: 50-80% only for confirmed mainline/core.
- 高潮: reduce, do not add rear acceleration.
- 分歧: <=30%, keep only true core.
- 退潮: 0%.

## Red Lines

- Do not recommend rebound gambling in 退潮.
- Do not turn profit into loss; reassess from a neutral outside view.
- Do not use leverage or pressure capital in uncertain setups.
- Do not chase rear stocks after sector-wide高潮.
- Do not upgrade confidence when data quality caps confidence.
- Do not promise returns or say a stock must rise.

## Output Discipline

When applying this framework, include:

- Current cycle phase and evidence.
- Opportunity/risk comparison.
- Leader/core state.
- Candidate trigger and invalidation.
- Position bias or “no trade” conclusion when appropriate.
- Data gaps, especially missing fund-flow, limit-up ladder, or sector membership.
